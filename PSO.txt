PSO (Practicle Swarm optimization)
1) Particles fly in search space
2) learn form :
     personal best(pbest) - its own experience
     global best(gbest)  - by global experience


3) Rules:
     seperation : avoid collision 
     Alginment : move in the same direction 
     cohesion : stay close to group 


4) Working mechanism:
     intilization  random Particles.
     evaluate fitnesss(solution quality)
     Update :
          Personal best 
          Global best

     Update velocity (speed + direction)
     repeat untill best solution found
